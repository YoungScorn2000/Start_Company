ABOUT US

Auto service START COMPANY from Kruševac, operating since 2007, is a regional leader in servicing, maintenance, and sale of spare parts for French vehicles.

The founder and owner Miljan Todorović jhas transferred his acquired knowledge and experience to his professional auto mechanic team, who enrich and improve it year after year up to this day.

Following the trends in maintenance and servicing of new passenger cars and commercial vehicles, we strive to always keep up with the times.

That is why since 2016, we have become a part of the MOTRIO SISTEM and network of authorized services, which enables us to become familiar with every new model of French cars that appears on the European market. We learn about all of its technical, service, and driving characteristics. Our auto mechanic team undergoes continuous training and education in the professional school centers of the MOTRIO SYSTEM located throughout the European Union.

The services we offer to our clients are in accordance with the recommendations and standards of car manufacturers.

Our services include regular and standard car maintenance, oil and filter replacement, all types of auto mechanic services, as well as auto electronics, electrical services and complete servicing and maintenance of air conditioning systems.

Within the Start Company car service, we also have a store selling original spare parts for all models of French vehicles like Pegueot, Reno, Renault Dacia and Citroen.

The large inventory of spare parts, as well as the latest tools, equipment and diagnostic devices that our car service has, give us an advantage in work and enable us to solve any problem or request in the shortest possible time and return the vehicle to the owner correctly, so that they can continue safe and secure driving with their beloved vehicle.

With our many years of professional work, quality, and speed, we have gained the trust of a large number of regular and occasional clients. Their trust, loyalty, and recommendations mean a lot to us and drive us to be even better and more professional.

Our guarantee for the performed work and original spare parts, together with the Service Booklet which we keep and stamp regularly, is an additional element for further loyalty.

Therefore, our list of loyal clients is getting longer and longer...
